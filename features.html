Our Foundation: Uncompromising Privacy

As outlined in our research, providing a secure and private tool is paramount. While the ultimate goal is on-device processing, our web-based tool uses a state-of-the-art hybrid encryption system to ensure your data's privacy is never compromised.

Hybrid Encryption: Security You Don't Have to Think About

When you submit text for analysis, it never leaves your browser in a readable format. We use a "double-lock" system (RSA and AES hybrid encryption) to guarantee that only you and our secure server can ever access the content, for the brief moment it's being analyzed.

How It Works: A Conceptual View

Think of it like sending a valuable item in a locked box. You lock the box (AES encryption), but then you put the key to that box inside a special, indestructible envelope that only our server can open (RSA encryption). Both are sent together, but neither can be compromised.

The Technical Deep-Dive

The entire request-response cycle is end-to-end encrypted. The temporary key used to encrypt and decrypt your data is generated in your browser, and the unencrypted version of it is never transmitted.

How We Achieve High Accuracy

A single model is not enough. Our strength lies in our multi-layered analysis.

1. Overall Classification

First, a powerful classifier model reads the entire text to provide a holistic AI probability score. This gives a strong baseline for the analysis.

2. Sentence-Level Highlighting

Next, we scan sentence by sentence, highlighting passages that are likely AI-generated versus human. This reveals the texture of the writing.

3. Deep Linguistic Analysis

Finally, we measure the vital signs of human writing: Perplexity (randomness), Variation (burstiness), Readability, and Lexical Richness. These metrics provide transparent, data-driven insights that contribute to the final score, which you can see in your results.
