Our Foundation: Uncompromising Privacy

A secure, private experience is our top priority. Our hybrid encryption system guarantees that your data remains protected from submission to analysis.

Hybrid Encryption: Security You Don’t Have to Think About

When you submit text, it never leaves your browser in a readable format. Using a dual-layer (RSA + AES) encryption model, your content stays private throughout the entire analysis process.

How It Works: A Conceptual View

Imagine sending a valuable item in a locked box. You lock it (AES), then seal the key in an envelope that only our secure server can open (RSA). Both travel together safely—yet remain indecipherable.

The Technical Deep-Dive

All communication is end-to-end encrypted. The temporary AES key is generated locally and never transmitted unencrypted, ensuring no intermediary ever sees your raw text.

How We Achieve High Accuracy

Our strength lies in layered intelligence — not a single model, but a coordinated network of insights.

1. Overall Classification

A deep classifier model scans the full text for AI patterns, establishing a strong probabilistic foundation.

2. Sentence-Level Highlighting

Sentence-by-sentence evaluation highlights likely AI-generated regions, revealing the structure and tone differences.

3. Deep Linguistic Analysis

We measure human writing “vital signs”: Perplexity, Burstiness, Readability, and Lexical Richness — ensuring transparent, data-driven accuracy.
