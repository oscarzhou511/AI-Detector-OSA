End-to-End Encryption Explained

When you submit text, a complex security process begins instantly. Your browser generates a temporary, secret key (AES) to encrypt your text. This secret key is then encrypted using our public key (RSA). Only our server, with its matching private key, can unlock the secret key to analyze your text. The results are then re-encrypted with the same secret key and sent back. At no point is your raw text transmitted or stored in a readable format.

How We Achieve High Accuracy

A single model is not enough. Our strength lies in our multi-layered analysis.

1. Overall Classification

First, a powerful classifier model reads the entire text to provide a holistic AI probability score. This gives a strong baseline for the analysis.

2. Sentence-Level Highlighting

Next, we scan sentence by sentence, highlighting passages that are likely AI-generated versus human. This reveals the texture of the writing.

3. Deep Linguistic Analysis

Finally, we measure the vital signs of human writing: Perplexity (randomness), Variation (burstiness), Readability, and Lexical Richness. These metrics provide transparent, data-driven insights that contribute to the final score, which you can see in your results.
